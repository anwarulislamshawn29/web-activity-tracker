Web Activity Tracker FAQ

Web Activity Tracker monitors the time you spend on websites, helping you understand your browsing habits. It tracks time per domain, provides daily/weekly/monthly summaries, and lets you export your data as a CSV file.

Click the extension icon to open the popup. You'll see a list of domains you've visited, along with the time spent. Use the tabs (Today, Yesterday, This Week, This Month, All Time) to filter your data. You can also export your data by clicking "Export Data" at the bottom.

The extension tracks time on websites using http or https protocols. It does not track time on Chrome internal pages (e.g., chrome://settings) or other protocols like file://.

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data is sent to external servers.

Yes, you can clear your tracked data by going to Chrome's extension settings. Navigate to chrome://extensions/, find Web Activity Tracker, and click "Remove" to delete all stored data. Alternatively, you can clear Chrome's local storage manually via Developer Tools.

No, your data is not shared with anyone. It remains on your device. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

In the extension popup, use the theme toggle at the top. Select "Light" or "Dark" to switch themes. Your preference is saved automatically.

The "Export Data" button downloads your tracked time data as a CSV file. It includes domains, time spent, and percentages for the selected time period (e.g., Today, All Time).

The extension tracks time spent on active tabs. It may not account for idle periods when a tab is open but not in focus. Time is updated every second while a tab is active.

You can support the developer by clicking the "Support My Work" link in the extension popup, which takes you to a Buy Me a Coffee page where you can make a donation.

Yes, you can close tabs directly from the extension. Each card in the popup has a close button (X icon) on the right. Click it to close all open tabs for that domain, and the display will update automatically.
